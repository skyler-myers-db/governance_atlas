Design a high-fidelity enterprise SaaS product screen for a Databricks-native metadata platform called “Governance Hub.”

Critical goals:
- It must look premium, modern, enterprise-grade, and highly credible.
- It must feel like a category-leading metadata/governance product that could beat OpenMetadata in first impression.
- It must NOT look like an internal dashboard, prototype, or student project.
- Governance Hub is Governance Hub-branded, not OpenMetadata-branded, but should match or exceed OpenMetadata-class information density, hierarchy, and polish.
- The design should immediately impress a CIO, data platform lead, or analytics leader and make them feel they need this product.

Visual direction:
- Clean, premium enterprise UI
- Crisp typography, dense but highly readable information architecture
- Sophisticated spacing rhythm
- Refined card system, subtle borders, restrained shadows
- Elegant neutral light theme with refined indigo/purple brand accents
- Soft, premium background treatment only if subtle and professional
- Zero childish styling, zero cartoon feel, zero flashy gimmicks
- Strong hierarchy and intentional use of contrast
- Beautiful right rails, drawers, tabs, tables, badges, and metadata chips
- Premium polished top nav and shell
- Feels like a best-in-class B2B SaaS product in 2026

Composition rules:
- Straight-on product screenshot
- No device mockup
- No fake browser chrome unless explicitly requested
- 16:10 desktop app view
- Show realistic, credible product data
- Use Databricks / Unity Catalog / governance / lineage / quality language naturally
- The UI must look shippable

Interaction design cues to imply:
- fast
- trustworthy
- intelligent
- enterprise
- deeply interconnected
- operationally useful
- high signal, low clutter

Important:
- Do not copy OpenMetadata branding or exact styling.
- Borrow the clarity, density, and maturity of OpenMetadata’s best surfaces, then elevate them.
- Make the page look like something prospects would screenshot and send internally with excitement.
